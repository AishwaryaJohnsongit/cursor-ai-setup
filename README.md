# LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

## Why I chose this topic

I chose LinkedIn organic content strategy for B2B SaaS because LinkedIn is a powerful platform for founders and professionals to build an audience, generate leads, and grow without relying on paid ads. This project focuses on understanding how real operators use content to drive business outcomes.

---

1. Alex Hormozi

## What I have collected

For each expert, I collected:

* Recent LinkedIn posts focused on strategy, frameworks, and actionable insights
* YouTube transcripts where available to understand deeper thinking and long-form content

The data is organized into:

* `/research/linkedin-posts/` → posts organized by expert
* `/research/youtube-transcripts/` → video transcripts
* `/research/other/` → additional materials

---

## How I collected the data

* LinkedIn posts were collected manually to ensure relevance and accuracy
* YouTube transcripts were collected using AI tools such as Claude/Codex or similar methods
* AI tools were also used to help structure and organize the data efficiently

---

## Goal of this research

The goal of this project is to build a high-quality dataset of real-world strategies used by B2B SaaS operators and content creators.

This will help in:

* identifying patterns in successful LinkedIn content
* understanding how audience converts into revenue
* building a practical playbook for content-driven growth
