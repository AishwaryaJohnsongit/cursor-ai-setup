Post 1 (Ideas > Execution in Modern Startup Era)

Link: https://www.youtube.com/@MyFirstMillionPodcast
 (reference source)

Content:
In today’s world, ideas matter more than ever.

Execution is still important, but it has become more accessible because of AI tools, no-code platforms, and distribution channels.

What is now rare is not execution.

It is:

identifying a valuable opportunity
spotting unmet demand early
framing the idea in a way people understand

A good idea can be tested quickly, built cheaply, and distributed instantly.

This changes the game.

The winners are not just builders anymore.

They are people who can consistently generate and refine high-leverage ideas.

Post 2 (Distribution is the Real Moat)

Link: https://www.youtube.com/@MyFirstMillionPodcast

Content:
Most people think the hardest part of business is building the product.

But in reality, building is becoming easier.

The harder problem is distribution.

If you cannot get attention, even a great product will fail.

If you can get attention, even a simple product can work.

This is why modern founders focus on:

content creation
audience building
storytelling
partnerships and leverage channels

Distribution compounds over time.

Once you have it, every new idea becomes easier to launch.

Without it, even great ideas die unnoticed.
