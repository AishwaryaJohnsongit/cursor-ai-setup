Post 1 (Content Distribution is as Important as Content Creation)
Date: ~recent
Link: https://www.linkedin.com/in/amandanat (LinkedIn activity feed – content distribution posts)
Content:
Most marketers spend all their time on content creation.
But creation is only half the job.
If you don’t distribute your content, it doesn’t matter how good it is.
Modern content strategy is not just about writing posts or publishing blogs.
It is about:


where the content shows up


how it gets reused across channels


how it is adapted for different audiences


how it reaches people beyond your existing followers


A single piece of content should not live in one place.
It should be:


repurposed


reshaped


redistributed


The companies that win with content are not just creators.
They are distribution systems.

Post 2 (Marketing Should Drive Revenue, Not Just Engagement)
Date: ~recent
Link: https://www.linkedin.com/in/amandanat
Content:
A lot of content marketing fails because it optimizes for the wrong metric.
Likes, impressions, and engagement feel good—but they don’t always translate to business impact.
Good marketing should be tied to outcomes:


pipeline


signups


revenue influence


customer quality


If content does not connect to business results, it becomes noise.
This is why modern marketers are shifting from:
“Did people like this?”
to
“Did this change behavior?”
Content is not the goal.
Business growth is the goal.
When marketing is aligned with revenue, every piece of content becomes more intentional.
