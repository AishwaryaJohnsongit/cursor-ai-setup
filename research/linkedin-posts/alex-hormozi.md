## Post 1 (Impact + Business)

Date: 3days ago

Link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/alexhormozi_you-can-make-a-real-impact-and-run-a-real-activity-7448761789703684097-QTFI

Content:
You can make a real impact and run a real business at the same time.

Helping people and making money are not opposites.

The best businesses:
- solve real problems
- deliver real results
- create value for others

When you focus on creating value first, revenue becomes a byproduct.

You don’t have to choose between purpose and profit.
You can build both together.

## Post 2 (Focus & Priorities)

Date:1week ago
Link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/alexhormozi_if-you-have-less-than-100k-in-your-bank-activity-7447614231849435137-4lax

Content:
If you have less than $100k in your bank account, your focus should not be on complex strategies.

It should be on:
- learning high-value skills
- working harder
- increasing your income

At the early stage, simplicity wins.

You don’t need:
- advanced investments
- complicated systems

You need:
- more income
- better skills
- consistent effort

Once you build a strong financial base, then you can optimize.
