Post 1 (AI Shift & Founder Thinking)
Date: ~recent
Link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/hnshah_i-wrote-this-because-i-think-a-lot-of-people-activity-7448025692325343232-zj_3
Content:
AI is changing how founders think and build.
A lot of people assume the main change is faster execution, but the real shift is deeper than that.
The real changes are happening in:


how quickly ideas can be tested


how much leverage a small team has


how important clarity of thinking becomes


how easy it is to build “average” products


Execution is no longer the bottleneck in many cases.
Thinking clearly, choosing the right problem, and communicating value have become more important than ever.
The winners in this new environment are not just builders.
They are:


clear thinkers


fast learners


strong communicators


people who can adapt quickly to new tools


AI does not remove the need for skill.
It raises the value of judgment.

Post 2 (Focus Shift in Modern SaaS)
Date: ~recent
Link: same post thread
Content:
Most SaaS founders still optimize for the wrong things.
They focus heavily on:


features


output


shipping speed


But in reality, the biggest growth constraints today are:


positioning clarity


messaging quality


distribution strategy


understanding user pain deeply


When these are unclear, even great products fail.
When these are strong, even simple products can win.
Modern SaaS growth is less about building more things.
It is about:
choosing the right problem
saying it clearly
reaching the right audience consistently
Clarity is becoming a competitive advantage.
