## Post 3 (FOUNDATION - Environment & output)

Date: 7 April 2026
Link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/justin-welsh_people-think-their-environment-shapes-them-activity-7445799358119288833-IGHl

Content:
People think their environment shapes them.

But they don’t realize:

They choose their environment.

And then their environment shapes them.

Choose wisely.

---

Takeaway:
Your environment directly impacts your consistency, thinking, and output.

For creators and SaaS builders, your “environment” includes:
- the content you consume
- the people you follow
- the platforms you spend time on

If you want better output, you must first design better inputs.

## Post 5 (RISK - Health & leverage)

Date: one week ago
Link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/justinwelsh_the-older-i-get-the-more-i-realize-that-activity-7446524130021076992-oVFG

Content:
The older I get, the more I realize how much work can impact your health when you're in the wrong environment.

Bad environments include:
- poor leadership
- high pressure
- constant stress
- unrealistic expectations

Over time, this leads to burnout and exhaustion.

The lesson is simple:
Don’t depend fully on one source of income or one job.

Build something of your own:
- an audience
- a skill
- a side income

That’s how you create leverage and options.
## Post 6 (MONETIZATION - Value capture)

Date: 2 weeks ago  
Link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/justinwelsh_you-only-capture-about-25-of-the-value-you-activity-7444349808343154688-fNLO

Content:
You only capture about 25% of the value you create in a traditional job.

The company captures the rest.

This is the standard trade in employment:
- You create value
- The company scales and monetizes it
- You receive a portion as salary or compensation

If you want to capture more of the value you create, you need ownership.

Because if you can create value for a company,
you can also create value for yourself.

The difference is not ability.
The difference is leverage and ownership.
